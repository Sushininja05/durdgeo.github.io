I'm Durdge-o! I'm an amateur game developer, programmer, artist, etc. I wear many hats but it's mainly just to fuel my hobbies, what I know I've picked up for funsies. I'm a big fan of fighting games, speedrunning, modding, freeware, source games, kusoge, blah blah. I used to do a lot of modding and romhacking, but I mainly just work on games now. If I'm not making something, I'm probably labbing a fighting game.

On that note, this website was created so I had a central hub for all my hobbies. It started as a project to fight my boredom but quicky ballooned as time went on. I don't update it much anymore, but it stays up with the hopes that it inspires others to make their own sites. Despite most of this site just being stolen html examples, I hope this inspires a few others to follow suit and break free from social media.

~Durdge-o
