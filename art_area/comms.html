Commissions.

I've been asked if I do commissions before, so I've setup this page for all your commission needs!
I've never done it in any real capacity, but I figured it'd be worth opening myself up to.
As such, here's all the useful information in case you were looking to commission me.

    Terms of Use/Service:

    (While I technically cannot enforce these rules, I am one guy after all, I would like it if you respected them.)

  • 1. Artwork, once paid for and received, is to be retained as my "creation".
    Meaning that I, Durdge-o, still own the art and can use it for future projects/portfolios/branding and other similar business.
  • 2. Artwork, once paid for and received, by the client/purchaser, can use it for anything as long as it is their own personal use.
    It may not be used for monetary gain, or sold as its own. (i.e. My artwork printed on shirts/stickers/keychains OR use in NFT/Crypto trading-- these use-cases are against my TOS.)
  • 3. However, Artwork, once paid for and received, by the client/purchaser, can be used for projects with goals of monetary gain or commercial use within reason.
    These exemptions from rule #2 are as follows: game development projects, album covers, comics, company logos, and other similar things. No royalties attached.
  • 4. Once paid, allow time for me to complete your commission, I do not have strict deadlines as this isn't very professional, but I will try to complete your commission as soon as possible.
  • 5. No refunds. I cannot refund you after payment is accepted and won't work on a piece until payment comes through.

    Art Guidelines:

    (A small series of guidelines for commissions from me, so that you may get a better picture of my art... both literally and figuratively.)

  • Will Draw:
    Just about anything, can't guarantee I will be good at it though.
    Will be more likely to draw art relating to things I like.
    Can do NSFW/Furry/Mecha, but its not my wheelhouse.
    		•
  • Won't Draw:
    Scat/Extreme Gore. Some cartoonier gore is still fine.
    I may still turn down art requests of questionable subject matter.
    		•

  • Pricing:
  • Doodles - $15~$20
    Prices go lower if the drawing is less complex, willing to do doodles of several characters at once at no additional cost.

  • Colored/Rendered Piece - $30~$45
    Prices get higher with big backgrounds and rendering. I don't particularly like coloring.
